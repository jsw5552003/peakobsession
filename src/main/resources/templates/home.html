Peak obsession is for those of us who love the mountains of the northeast. I always tracked my hiking lists on spreadsheets and my girlfriend would do the same and we when we wanted to do a hike we would have to do a complicated cross reference to try and figure out which mountains we both needed for which lists and what months and then when we hiked with friends with their own list goals...needless to say it became a complicated affair. So Peak Obsesion was born! This site allows you to enter your hikes, check your progress on lists, and then view which hikes you and a friend both need for lists. Hopefully you find it useful!
